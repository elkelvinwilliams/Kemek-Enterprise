A family business built on trust, returns & real homes.

Kemek Enterprise is a UK family-run property company. We work at both ends of the market — securing the right deals for investors and landlords, and creating quality homes for the people who live in them.

Whether you want to invest without lifting a finger, sell quickly, source your next property, or simply find somewhere to call home, our team manages the whole process with care, discretion and a long-term view.

What We Do

Six ways we work in property

From a single sale to a fully managed portfolio — pick the level of involvement that suits you.

Buy & Sell

We acquire and sell residential property — securing fair value whether you're buying your next home or releasing equity quickly.

Rent-to-Rent & Lettings

We rent properties, add value, and let them well — giving landlords guaranteed peace of mind and tenants a home that's properly run.
