The Kemek Story

Built by family, for the long term

Kemek Enterprise began with a simple belief: that property should work for everyone it touches — the investor, the landlord, the contractor, and above all the person who calls it home.

As a family-run business, we don't chase the quick win. We build relationships, do the right thing when no one's watching, and treat every client and every property as if it were our own. That's how families work — and that's how we work.

Today we operate across the full spectrum of property: buying and selling, rent-to-rent and lettings, sourcing deals, fully managed investing, and a premium accommodation portfolio for professionals. Tomorrow, we move into social housing — because the ultimate measure of a property business is the lives it improves.

At the heart of it all is EA Interiors — our in-house design and development studio led by Emmanuella Akua, principal developer and interior designer — making sure every property we touch is finished to a standard we'd be proud to call home.
